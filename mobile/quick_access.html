How Jarvis Works

Architecture
Jarvis has two parts: your phone acts as the sensor and interface, while your desktop PC is the brain that does all the thinking.

Intelligence
When you ask Jarvis a question, it routes to the best available AI model. For privacy-sensitive queries, it uses a local model that never sends data to the cloud.

Memory
Jarvis remembers your conversations, facts about you, and builds a knowledge graph of connections between concepts.

Proactive Engine
Jarvis doesn't just wait for commands -- it monitors your schedule, habits, and context to provide helpful nudges.

Security
All communication between your phone and desktop is encrypted and signed. Your data stays on your devices.
